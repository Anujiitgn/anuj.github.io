A
Anuj Prakash Kushwaha
Research Scholar · Dept. of Earth Science
AB-4/326, IIT Gandhinagar, Gujarat

I am an Earth Science researcher at the intersection of water resources and sustainable development. My work uses multi-hydrological modelling and hydrometeorological analysis — integrating observed, satellite, and reanalysis data — to understand hydrological processes shaped by climate change and human activities.

I employ models including VIC, CLM, Noah-MP, CWatM and H08 to quantify the human footprint of irrigation, groundwater abstraction, and reservoir operations on Indian river basins. I have also contributed high-resolution datasets including the Drought Atlas of India and INDmet.

Supervisor: Prof. Vimal Mishra · Expected submission: April 2026
